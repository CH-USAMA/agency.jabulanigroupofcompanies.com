The Challenge

One Group, Many Businesses — One Clear Story

The Jabulani Group operates across multiple sectors — mining, technology, retail, and more. They needed a central online presence that clearly communicated their full scope and authority as a group, while also directing visitors to each individual business.

The previous online presence was fragmented and didn't convey the scale or professionalism of the organisation.

Our Solution

A Corporate Hub with a Clear Hierarchy

We designed a clean, authoritative corporate website that leads with the group's story and values, then presents each division as its own card — with a unique colour identity, brief description, and link to its dedicated site.

The design is deliberately understated and professional — letting the businesses speak for themselves.

What We Built

Key Features

Multi-Division Architecture

Each business division has its own section with distinct visual identity — cohesive as a group, distinct individually.

Clean Corporate Design

Minimal, professional aesthetic that conveys trust and authority — suitable for B2B relationships and investor communication.

Inter-site Navigation

Smart linking between the group site and each division's dedicated website — one ecosystem, multiple destinations.

Fully Responsive

Looks equally strong on desktop, tablet, and mobile — for executives, partners, and customers alike.

SEO for the Brand

Optimised for searches about the Jabulani Group and its leadership — building brand authority in local search results.

Fast & Reliable

Lightweight HTML/CSS/JS — no heavy frameworks, no unnecessary dependencies. Loads instantly everywhere.

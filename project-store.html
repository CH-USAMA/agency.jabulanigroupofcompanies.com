The Challenge

Selling Online — Without Compromising on Quality

The Jabulani Group wanted to expand into e-commerce and sell products directly to customers across South Africa. They needed a store that looked premium, loaded fast, and made the buying journey as smooth as possible.

The challenge: build something that handled product management, secure payments, and order tracking — without relying on expensive third-party platforms that take a cut of every sale.

Our Solution

A Custom Store Built to Sell

We built a fully custom e-commerce store with a clean, modern design that feels premium without being cold. Product pages, category filters, a persistent cart, and a streamlined checkout flow — all optimised for conversion.

Payment integration was set up securely, and the admin panel lets the team manage products, orders, and inventory without any technical knowledge.

What We Built

Key Features

Product Catalogue

Organised product pages with images, descriptions, variants, and pricing — easy to browse and search.

Cart & Checkout

Persistent cart with quantity controls, discount codes, and a streamlined checkout that minimises drop-off.

Secure Payments

Integrated payment gateway with SSL encryption — customers can pay with confidence.

Filters & Search

Category filtering and keyword search help shoppers find exactly what they need, faster.

Admin Panel

A simple backend interface for managing products, orders, inventory, and customer records — no tech skills needed.

Mobile-Optimised

Designed mobile-first — most South African shoppers browse on their phones, so this was non-negotiable.

Jabulani Point of Sale

A fully custom retail management system — built for speed, built for scale.

Client Jabulani Group of Companies
Industry Retail / Business Software
Location Eastern Cape, South Africa
Live System pos.jabulanigroupofcompanies.co.za
The Challenge

Managing a Retail Business Without the Right Tools

The Jabulani Group needed to manage inventory, track sales, and monitor staff activity across their retail operations — but off-the-shelf POS solutions were either too expensive, too complex, or simply didn't fit the South African market.

Manual processes were leading to stock discrepancies, slow checkouts, and poor visibility into which products were performing.

Our Solution

A Custom POS Built Exactly to Spec

We built a bespoke point-of-sale system from the ground up — designed around how the Jabulani retail team actually works. The system includes a real-time dashboard, full inventory management, staff logins with role-based access, and exportable reports.

The dark UI is fast to navigate on any device, keeping queues short and staff confident.

What We Built

Key Features

Point of Sale

Fast, intuitive checkout interface with barcode scanning, receipt printing, and multiple payment types.

Inventory Management

Real-time stock tracking with low-stock alerts, supplier records, and purchase order management.

Sales Dashboard

Visual reporting on daily, weekly, and monthly sales — broken down by product, category, and staff member.

Multi-User Access

Role-based login system — cashiers, managers, and admins each see only what they need.

Reporting & Export

Generate and export reports to CSV for accounting, tax, and business planning purposes.

Secure & Reliable

Hosted on a secure server with daily backups, SSL encryption, and audit logs for every transaction.

Jabulani Crush & Quarry

A professional digital presence for an Eastern Cape mining company — built to rank, built to impress.

Client Jabulani Group of Companies
Industry Open-cast Mining
Location Tsolo, Eastern Cape, SA
Live Site jabulaniquarriestsolo.co.za
The Challenge

An Industry Giant with No Online Presence

Jabulani Crush & Quarry had been operating in Tsolo for years — supplying aggregate to construction companies across the Eastern Cape — but had zero digital footprint. Potential clients couldn't find them, and the company relied entirely on word-of-mouth referrals.

The brief was clear: build a site that would rank on Google for quarry-related searches in the Eastern Cape, showcase their services and capabilities, and make it easy for contractors to get in touch.

Our Solution

SEO-First Design, Built to Last

We designed and built a fully custom website from scratch — no templates. The site was structured with clear service pages optimised for location-based search terms, a photo gallery showcasing their fleet and operations, and a dark mode UI that sets them apart visually.

Every page was built with semantic HTML, schema markup, and mobile performance in mind from day one.

What We Built

Key Features

Dark Mode UI

A bold, dark-themed design with red accents that matches the industrial nature of the business.

Photo Gallery

A filterable gallery showcasing equipment, operations, and completed projects to build trust with contractors.

Local SEO

Optimised for Eastern Cape quarry keywords, Google Business Profile integration, and schema markup.

Mobile-First

Fully responsive and fast-loading on all devices — essential for contractors browsing on-site.

Contact & Quote Forms

Multiple contact touchpoints make it simple for potential clients to request quotes or ask questions.

Performance Optimised

Compressed assets, lazy-loaded images, and clean code deliver fast load times even on mobile data.

The Results

Online. Discoverable. Growing.

Mobile Optimised for all screen sizes
